Glucan depolymerization

Glucan depolymerization was measured by a new 13C pool dillution assay. Method and results were extensively reported in [Leitner et al.(2011)Leitner, and the Crew]. We compared glucan depolymerization rates presented there to respiration rates in this publication. To even out incertenties in the absolute quantification for both methods and to focus on comparing differences within harvests and litter types, we calculated the ratio between respiration and glucan depolymerization.
